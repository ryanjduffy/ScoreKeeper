Score Keeper is the premiere application on webOS for keeping score of your games and activities. It's great for child and recreational games when traditional scoreboards are unavailable. Score Keeper features a large, high-contrast display to ensure maximum visibility. It includes 8 customizable boards with clocks (where appropriate) and more to come as downloadable content.

With Score Keeper, you'll never be caught asking, "What's the score?"
